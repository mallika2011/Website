More about me



Hi! I am Mallika Subramanian. I was born in Kuwait and have spent a majority of my life there. Having been brought up entirely in Kuwait I gained an excellent mix of cultures and traditions. I'm from Delhi Public School, Kuwait and currently an undergraduate student at IIIT-H persuing CSE. This is one of my first projects for web development. It has been an experience learning and implementing it.



I am a passionate bharatanatyam dancer, trained for fourteen years and have learnt carnatic vocals as well. Both of these continue to be an integral part of me. I have been a part of several productions of my dance academy, Kinkini. Alongside these art forms, I enjoy public speaking, typography and baking! I have also been fortunate to have the opportunity to participate as a delegate at the Harvard MUN conducted in India.
